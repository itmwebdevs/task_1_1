Angelina Jolie

June 4, 1975

Angelina Jolie is an American actress, filmmaker, and humanitarian. She was born on June 4, 1975, in Los Angeles, California. Jolie is one of the world's most renowned actresses, known for her versatility and intensity in her performances. She has received numerous awards and accolades, including an Academy Award and three Golden Globe Awards.

"Jolie is emerging as one of the great wild spirits of current movies, a loose cannon who somehow has deadly aim."
